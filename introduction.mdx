## What is Edens?

Edens provides efficient tools to help you run your business as an independent freelancer or a team, allowing you to focus on your creative activities.

By using essential features such as project billing management, time tracking, and invoicing, you can improve the billing experience for your business and clients.


### Where to start?

- Choose a topic that interests you from the left menu.
- Use the search function in the top menu.
- Read our guides for more information.
